# Green Logistics: Transport Routing Optimization for Reforestation 

Published academic article on [Applied Mathematics in Industry](https://www.springerprofessional.de/en/applied-mathematics-in-industry/51900042) (Springer) in 2026

Made with collaboration of a Mexican reforestal organization, mixed-integer linear programming was applied to the optimization of a Vehicle Routing Problem (VRP), with the purpose of minimizing the distance and time associated with the delivery of reforestation plants.
A heuristic was also created to solve the problem almost optimally with a fraction of the total time.
